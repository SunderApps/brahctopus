The reaction from Locus was clear, they had met before. The Mothership instantly changed demeanors, withdrawing cannons and deploying a massive shield. It became clear the Mothership had been looking for Locus all along.

A fierce battle ensued, but Locus was unable to penetrate the shields, even with the rest of Brahctopus. Now, the band has decided to launch an internal attack on the Mothership. Once inside, they can disable the shield generator. That is, once they defeat those protecting it...
