Bio

Brahctopus is a 7 piece rock outfit, hailing from Pittsburgh, playing originals from several genres spanning jam-rock, psychedelic, reggae and funk. The inspiration is drawn from across the board, no time period excluded. With surreal lyrics, mind bending solos, and feel good rhythms; the goal is to bring the audience an unforgettable and innovative music experience to broaden the imagination. Brahctopus consists of ukulele, synthesizers, electric guitar, acoustic guitar, key-tar, bass, drums and astounding vocal harmonies. Brahctopus is currently working on their debut psychedelic-jam album to be released on all outlets early 2019. Keep it Squiggly!
